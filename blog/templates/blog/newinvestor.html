What is Cryptocurrency?

Their is a history associated with every altcoin theirs a method to every script. Spend a proper amount of time educating yourself of the norms of this industry before jumping in. Follow the trends daily & get engaged in growing your capabilities as a Investor. In this video we talk about the federal reserve & how cryptocurrency holds its valuable.

Building a Dynamic Portfolio

Surrounding yourself around other smart people with experience, invite different prospectives & contribute value in-order to receive value. Learn how to structure & maintain an online portfolio that works and understand the dynamics of splitting up a protfolio.

Only Trade Companies You Trust

Narrow down your investments only to good projects, understanding the fundamentals of a project what the hell are you investing in? Why is this coin going to hold & grow value? Are their driving forces within the project that directly correlate to the value of the coin? These are things you need to know, 2018 was the year of consolidation. Save yourself from investing into "shitcoins" & loss can be limited if you narrow your scope based on fundamentials

Create Rules

Hold yourself & your investments to a set of rules & alter these rules depending on the results you find. I suggest to split your portfolio up into different sections & moving your holdings in each section based on market conditions.What overall criteria needs to be set for you to enter or exit the market? Learn to hedge & diversify, as some times no investment or shorting is the best option.

Go in Expecting to Learn

Expect to lose some Trades especially in the beginning as you are just learning, with the mindset get your feet wet by starting with a small investment look to prove the concepts of overall trading strategies based on criteria you set plan to experience the cycles of a bull and bear market. Before you feel comfortable ramming up the automation trading systems you build.

Learn to Hedge

If you are entering in a investment make sure you have a back up plan. Holding a large position in USD when trading the market is smart. Set DCA rules, stop loss, learn to short basically don't put your eggs one basket. Create the ability to move funds if need be by focusing on diversification & a quick pull out game.

Reference Price Data

Learn that in a bear or bull market Bitcoin is King, if btc is sending out red alert learn to protect yourself by limiting your altcoin holdings. I recommend watching outside markets & making sure to follow politics & news concerning your investments.
