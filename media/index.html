Building a Dynamic Portfolio 

Learn how to build & manage your own portfolio & move assets around online. 
Learn the rules & Techniques associated with automation trading.

Start with only Invest  Time

Learn the basics of automation trading by only spending your time. Limit Risk by starting small their will always be another opportunity. Invest in educating yourself before you enter in this market. The goal is 1-2 hours of mainance a day in return you learn how to grow your own portfolio.

Figure out the Fundamentials

Markets work under the rule of gravity as in the real world, stock market & crypto market what goes up must go down. Learn the driving factors that make this market tick & ONLY invest in safe projects you follow & understand.

Learn How to Roll the Dice 

You can only prep so much eventually you will reach a point where you are ready to invest. My suggestion is start small prove your investment strategy & see if you can commit to the attention that is required to be a sucessful automation trading.  

Analyze & Tweak the System 

The point is to build a portfolio that works for you. The only way to achieve this is by learning from your actions on what is working & isn't. Focus on the numbers & learn to adapt to the market. Expect to make bad calls & plan for a learning curve theirs no easy way out. How you learn from your mistakes & adapt will determine your trading capabilities.
